About Me

Angelina Jolie is an acclaimed American actress, filmmaker, and humanitarian. With a career spanning decades, she has delivered powerful performances in films and received numerous awards. Beyond her acting prowess, Jolie is known for her extensive humanitarian work and advocacy for refugee rights, women's rights, and child welfare.
